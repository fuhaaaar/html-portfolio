The Best Movies According To Fuhar

My top 3 movies of all-time

My Neighbour Totoro

A comforting studio ghibli film , takes you to the beautiful ghibli world.The characters are close to home and give a sense of safe environment and comfort to the watchers.The animation is top notch.

Jab We Met

A bollywood rom-com about a guy lost in life after his mom abandons their family who coincidently meets a full of life sunshine-girl on a train.An amazing story with jam-wothy songs, Jab We Met easily makes it into every bollywood fan's top 5.A must watch.

Hereditary

A bone-chilling psyhological horror based on a satanic cult.About a family who suffers a traumatic event which leaves them all devastated , things start to go downhill after the mother realises that her mom was a part of a cult and decides to dig into it.It will leave you stunned by the end.
